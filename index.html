About Me

Hi! My name is Zach Clay. I’m 19 years old and I’m from Savannah, GA. I’m currently studying at Clemson University’s College of Engineering, Computing, and Applied Sciences. I am working towards a degree in Computer Information Systems. To tell you a little bit about myself, here are some of the things I enjoy and am passionate about: First, I love serving the Lord. I was saved by the grace of God at a young age of 7 years old. Although my walk with Christ hasn’t been perfect, I strive everyday to go deeper in my relationship with the Lord and glorify Him through my actions. I love spreading the gospel to people I come in contact with and seek to disciple others into a relationship with Christ. One way that I do this is through serving on Clemson FCA’s worship team. At Clemson, we have one of the largest FCA ministries in the country. I have the amazing opportunity to serve weekly as a guitarist in order to further His kingdom. It is always the highlight of my week. I am also a brother in Clemson BYX (Brothers Under Christ), a fraternity on Clemson’s campus. We meet weekly to fellowship and strengthen each other’s faith. I enjoy critical thinking and using my knowledge of coding and programming to solve problems. I constantly look forward to learning more and becoming a better computer scientist. Some of my other hobbies include hanging out with friends, spending time with family, playing sports, snowboarding, and hiking.
